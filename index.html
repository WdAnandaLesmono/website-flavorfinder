About Us

Our application aims to assist individuals, especially stay-at-home parents, in overcoming the challenge of deciding daily meal menus and reducing food waste. We provide a solution through a mobile application that allows users to plan meals more efficiently. The problem we aim to address is the lack of culinary inspiration and inefficient use of food ingredients. By offering a user-friendly interface and a variety of recipe options, our app helps users explore new meal ideas and make the most out of available ingredients. Moreover, our platform facilitates meal planning by providing personalized suggestions based on dietary preferences, allergies, and available ingredients, thus streamlining the cooking process and minimizing food wastage. We understand the importance of convenience in today's fast-paced lifestyle. Hence, our app enables users to conveniently search for recipes by taking a photo of the ingredients they have, leveraging image recognition technology.

How to use our app

Step 1

If you already have an account, please log in using your credentials. If you don't have an account yet, you can easily register by providing your email address and creating a password. This will give you full access to all features of the app.

Step 2

Once logged in, you will be able to browse through our extensive collection of recipes. Whether you're looking for breakfast ideas, quick snacks, or gourmet dinners, you'll find a wide variety of options to choose from.

Step 3

Use the app's photo scan feature to take a picture of any food item. The app will analyze the image and suggest recipes that can be made using the ingredients identified in the photo. This is a great way to discover new dishes and make the most of the ingredients you have on hand.

Step 4

Found a recipe you love? You can bookmark it for easy access later. Simply click on the bookmark icon on the recipe page, and it will be saved to your personal collection. This way, you can quickly find your favorite recipes whenever you need them.
